An introduction to Wireframe

A wireframe is a simple visual guide that shows the basic structure and layout of a webpage or app.
It outlines where key elements like buttons, images, and text will go, without focusing on design details like colors or fonts.
It's used early in the design process to plan and communicate ideas.
