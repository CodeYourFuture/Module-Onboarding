Iran is a Middle Eastern nation characterized by its rich history, diverse culture, and significant role in global affairs. Located in southwestern Asia, it's known for its mountainous terrain, arid landscapes, and for bordering the Persian Gulf and the Caspian Sea. The country's history stretches back millennia, with influences from various empires and civilizations. Iran is a complex and diverse population, reflecting its diverse cultural heritage.
