What is the purpose of a README file?

An introductory document for a project, software, or dataset, providing users with essential information about its purpose, usage, and how to get started

What is the purpose of a wireframe?

To establish the basic structure and layout of a website or application before visual design and content are added. Also, to provide a visual understanding of a page early in a project to get stakeholder and project team approval before the creative phase gets underway
