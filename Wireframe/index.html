Wireframe

A wireframe is a simplified visual blueprint of a webpage or app, outlining its layout, structure, and functionality without detailed design elements, serving as a guide for design and development.

What is the purpose of a wireframe?

Wireframe serve as essential tools in structural planning for digital interfaces, helping designers organize and arrange components to create a logical framework. They illustrate user flow by mapping how users navigate through the interface and access features, enhancing the planning of user interactions. Additionally, wireframes facilitate communication among stakeholders, designers, and developers by providing a unified visual language for design ideas and concepts.
